About The Vault Dance

The Vault Dance is a passionate and welcoming dance academy that has been running successfully for the past 5 years. We offer a space where dancers of all ages and skill levels can grow, express themselves, and enjoy the art of movement. Whether it's hip hop, ballet, Bollywood, or salsa, our classes are full of energy, learning, and fun.

Our lead trainer, Vishal Singh, has been teaching dance for over 10 years. He brings experience, creativity, and dedication to every session, helping students build both skill and confidence. We also specialize in wedding choreography and annual function training, helping you make your big moments even more special with unforgettable performances.

Our lead trainer, Vishal Singh, brings passion, precision, and a unique vibe that inspires every dancer.
